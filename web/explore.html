SIMyCity checks what it needs nearby, what it would induce, the standoffs in the way, and whether stakeholders even think it should be built — using live OpenStreetMap data plus an open model you can edit.

Everything here runs in your browser. The thresholds live in data_sources/layers.yaml — change one and this changes.
